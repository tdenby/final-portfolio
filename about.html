About Me

I'm a second-year master's student at the University of Michigan studying user experience design and research. I have a background in understanding human behavior, having studied cognitive science as an undergrad. I love problem-solving and working with others! When I'm not designing, I enjoy a variety of hobbies, including; playing videogames, playing guitar, going to music shows, and being a tv/movie snob. Growing up, you could have witnessed me doing nearly a dozen different musicals, and so although I no longer do theater, I still am very passionate about music. I also am passionate about cooperative living, having lived in a coop for 4 years now. Finally, I have 3 pet rats that I love!

I've been an avid tech fan since I was little, constantly reading about the newest tech products and tinkering with any software application that I found interesting. When Google released its first Material Design guidelines, I became enamored with design, and it became my mission to learn how to make aesthetically-pleasing and easy-to-use designs! I've been using my time at the University of Michigan's MSI program to learn all I can, from different coding languages to research methods and advanced graphic design principles. I've even gotten to use some of these skills volunteering on the marketing team of the Inter-Cooperative Council of Ann Arbor.
